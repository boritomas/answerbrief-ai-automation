# Codex Instructions

You are working on AnswerBrief AI, a small MVP for role-specific interview prep.

Primary customer:
Experienced professionals preparing for serious interviews in telecom, federal, finance, audit, compliance, operations, product, and leadership roles.

Product promise:
Resume + job posting in. Interview brief out.

Rules:
- Keep the app simple.
- Do not add complex architecture without a clear need.
- Do not collect sensitive personal data.
- Do not use employer-confidential materials.
- Do not automate LinkedIn posting without review.
- Keep package prices visible and easy to update.
- Use environment variables for all secrets.
- Add tests when logic becomes non-trivial.
- Explain changes in plain English.

First priority:
Make the checkout and intake flow reliable.
