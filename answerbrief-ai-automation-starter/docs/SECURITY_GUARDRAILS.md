# Security and Privacy Guardrails

## Do not collect

- Passwords
- SSNs
- Bank account numbers
- Full card numbers
- Employer-confidential documents
- Internal company systems screenshots
- Proprietary strategy documents
- Medical, legal, or immigration documents unless specifically needed and handled with care

## Allowed materials

- Resume
- Public job posting
- Candidate-written career notes
- Public company information
- Public agency or employer descriptions
- Interview date and format
- Candidate-approved work examples

## Delivery disclaimer

AnswerBrief AI provides interview preparation support. It does not guarantee interviews, offers, promotions, or employment outcomes.

## Employer separation

Do not use Verizon systems, confidential information, templates, documents, screenshots, internal data, or work time for AnswerBrief AI.
