I’m testing a new service called AnswerBrief AI.

The idea is simple.

You send your resume and the job posting. We turn it into a role-specific interview prep package that helps you understand what the interview is likely testing, how to position your experience, and which examples you should use.

The first version is focused on telecom, federal, finance, audit, compliance, operations, product, and leadership roles.

Each prep package includes:

- Opening pitch
- Resume-to-role alignment
- Likely interview questions
- STAR story bank
- Technical prep
- Weak spots to tighten
- Final 24-hour prep sheet
